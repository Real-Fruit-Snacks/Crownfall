Crownfall is a pixel-art medieval wave-defense incremental. You command a row of heroes on a stone wall. Goblins, chargers, archers, healers, witches, and crowned bosses pour out of the field on the right. Hold them off as long as you can — every death banks Research Points and Crowns that permanently upgrade your fortress.

You will die. A lot. Every run makes the next one stronger.

Latest Update — AFK Toolkit, Soft-Lock Fix & Smarter Commanders

Direct follow-up to rofltastic's review. Six commits covering the wave-41 soft-lock story, a full unattended-play toolkit for idle-game fans, the busted auto-upgrade AI, and a UI polish pass to match. Nothing is nerfed from the enemy side — the tools got better instead.

Anti-soft-lock bundle

  • Damage upgrades scale now. Was flat +1 per level (irrelevant at late waves); now +2% of the hero's base damage per level. 50 levels = 2× base, 100 levels = 3× base. Legacy heroes migrate cleanly.
  • Bosses are immune to knockback. Cannon "concussive" and crossbow "heavy" builds no longer shove bosses backward faster than they can advance — that was the wave-41 stalemate path. Minions still eat the full 10px shove.
  • Sell Hero button. Every filled slot panel gets a Sell · +Ng button that refunds 50% of total gold spent (hire + upgrades + imbues). Confirm dialog so misclicks can't nuke a carry. Mid-run pivots are finally real.
  • Priority targeting. Each hero gets a chip-button row on its panel: Closest, To Wall, Low HP, High HP, Tanks (max HP). CC-heavy setups can now explicitly focus bosses/elites instead of shoving minions indefinitely.

AFK / Idle toolkit

  • Auto-Dismiss Events (Settings). Mid-run event popups auto-close after 5 / 15 / 30 / 60 seconds. Dismissing picks no option — no reward, no penalty. Clicking any option cancels the timer and resolves normally. 15-second default. Opt-in.
  • AFK Auto-Restart (Settings). If a wave stays "active" longer than 1 / 2 / 5 / 10 minutes, the run ends, banks any RP earned, and starts a fresh run. Commanders re-fill slots automatically. No cap on restart frequency — a long-running tab genuinely farms RP overnight.
  • Offline progress — closes the loop for players who close the browser. Each finished run records its RP-per-minute into a smoothed rolling average; on next boot, time away × that rate is granted as RP with a welcome-back toast. No cap per spec.
  • Wall Breached auto-retry. When AFK Auto-Restart is on, the gameover screen shows an Auto-retry in Ns · click any button to cancel pulsing countdown (10s). Survives wall-breach, not just wave-stall — the overnight loop keeps rolling either way.

Commander auto-upgrade, rewritten

  • Round-robin distribution across heroes. Used to drain the treasury into slot 0 before anyone else got a turn. Now each eligible commander gets one upgrade per cycle in its priority order, cycles repeat until gold runs out. Matched-preset commanders end with identical level counts instead of one carry and three scrubs.
  • Speed-priority actually works now. Hidden typo on the progress-reset path (upgradePreset = 'roundRobin') silently fell through to the dmg-first default, so commanders set to SPD or RNG priority were quietly dumping all gold into damage. Now resets to no auto-upgrade — the player picks.

UI polish

  • Hero XP text finally readable. The N / N XP numbers on the level bar were dark brown on a gradient that hit near-black on the empty portion — effectively invisible. Now light cream with a 1px black outline, readable over both gold fill and dark empty.
  • Target priority is a chip row, not a <select>. The native dropdown stuck out. Replaced with 5 pixel-art chip buttons that match the commander preset styling; click to set, active highlight moves live.

Older update — Prestige Tree Expansion  (click to expand)

The prestige tree went from 10 bonuses to 20, with a full reshuffle of what the late game looks like. New lategame-scaling damage, new compounding shard returns, a roaming pixel-art war wolf, comeback mechanics, a node-preservation modal, and two bug fixes. No enemy nerfs — the wall stays real, but so does the door next to it.

10 new prestige bonuses

  • Empire's Reach (8 shards × 3) — +0.5% hero damage per wave cleared past 20 per stack. Maxed, your wave-50 army hits +45% harder than your wave-20 army.
  • Endless March (12 shards) — +1 bonus Crown Shard per lifetime prestige cycle. Later prestiges pay bigger than earlier ones.
  • Hunter's Mark (8 shards) — first hit on each enemy marks it; every hit after deals +15% damage.
  • Bank Vault (15 shards) — 25% of your unspent gold at run end carries to next run's starting purse.
  • Promotion (6 shards) — every prestige, one random hero type gains a permanent +10% damage stack. Uncapped lottery pull per prestige.
  • Skill Mastery (8 shards × 3) — -10% active skill cooldown per stack.
  • Phoenix Down (18 shards) — first wall-HP-zero each run revives the wall to 50% HP.
  • Dual Imbue (25 shards) — heroes can stack a second element on top of their primary. Both trigger on direct hits, splash, and mage chains.
  • The Hound (25 shards) — unlocks a feral war-wolf that roams the battlefield. Pixel-art wolf sprite with pointed ears, raised tail, glowing amber eye.
  • Ancestry (20 shards × 3) — on prestige, preserve 1/2/3 currently-owned research nodes across the wipe. Picker modal opens at prestige time.

Existing prestige bonuses buffed

  • Sharpened Edge now stacks up to 10 (was 5). Max damage bonus: +25% → +50%.
  • Crowned Legacy doubled: +1% per prior prestige per stack → +2%.
  • Shard formula rewards depth — sqrt(totalRp/50) + floor(deepestWave/10). Pushing past your previous best pays extra shards per 10-wave milestone.

Discoverability fixes

  • Prestige Unlock toast — big gold ♚ PRESTIGE UNLOCKED popup on first cross of 500 lifetime RP.
  • Pulsing pause-button badge with gold glow + ♛ crown until you open prestige once.
  • Endless Zone milestone — clearing wave 50 pops a 👑 ENDLESS ZONE REACHED toast. Every wave from 50 onward banks 1.5× Research Points.
  • Prestige screen surfaces Promotions + depth bonus in the blurb so you see them at a glance.

Bug fixes from player reviews

  • Bard phantom tooltip fixed — no more "Unlock Bard Alchemy" message for a node that never existed.
  • Projectile hitbox scales with enemy size — ram bosses get a 9px hitbox (was 5px flat). Fixes fast-attacking heroes skimming past at certain angles.

Controls

  • Click — hire heroes, buy upgrades, collect coins, tap enemies
  • Hold on an enemy — keep damaging it while pressed
  • Swipe over coins — auto-collect
  • Double-click WAVE block — skip to the next wave
  • 1–5 — cast skill in slot N
  • Q — end run, bank Research Points
  • R — open research book
  • Space — pause/resume
  • M — mute sound

Tech notes

Single self-contained index.html. No installs, no accounts, no analytics. Runs offline once loaded. Progress is saved in your browser's localStorage — clearing site data wipes your save. Use the in-game Settings → Export Save to back it up.
